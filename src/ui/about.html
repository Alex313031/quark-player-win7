About Quark Player

Quark Player is a fork of ElectronPlayer which is itself a fork of (the same author's) Netflix app. I have updated Electron from 12.0.11 to , updated the name and icon, added more streaming services, added more menu commands, and updated npm packages including the Ad Blocker (by Cliqz).
